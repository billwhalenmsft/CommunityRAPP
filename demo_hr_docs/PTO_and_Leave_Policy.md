# Contoso Corporation — PTO & Leave Policy
**Document:** HR-POL-002  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025  
**Owner:** Human Resources

---

## Overview

Contoso provides a comprehensive leave program to support employee wellbeing and work-life balance. This policy applies to all full-time regular employees. Part-time and contract employees should refer to their individual agreements.

---

## 1. Paid Time Off (PTO)

Contoso uses a **unified PTO bank** that covers vacation, personal days, and non-illness-related absences.

### 1.1 PTO Accrual Schedule

| Years of Service | Annual PTO Accrual | Accrual per Pay Period |
|---|---|---|
| 0–1 years | 10 days (80 hrs) | 3.08 hrs |
| 1–3 years | 15 days (120 hrs) | 4.62 hrs |
| 3–7 years | 20 days (160 hrs) | 6.15 hrs |
| 7+ years | 25 days (200 hrs) | 7.69 hrs |

- PTO begins accruing on day 1 of employment.
- Employees may not take PTO before it is accrued during the first 90 days.
- PTO accrual is paused during unpaid leaves of absence.

### 1.2 PTO Cap
Employees may carry a maximum of **1.5× their annual accrual** into the next calendar year. Accrual stops once the cap is reached.

### 1.3 Requesting PTO
- Submit requests via **Workday** at least 5 business days in advance for requests of 3+ consecutive days.
- Same-day or urgent requests require direct manager approval.
- Managers should respond to PTO requests within 2 business days.

### 1.4 PTO Payout on Separation
Accrued, unused PTO is paid out upon termination of employment in accordance with state law.

---

## 2. Sick Leave

Employees earn **1 hour of sick leave for every 30 hours worked**, up to a maximum of **48 hours (6 days) per year**.

- Sick leave may be used for the employee's own illness, medical appointments, or to care for an immediate family member.
- Sick leave does not carry over year-to-year beyond 48 hours total.
- Employees absent for 3+ consecutive days due to illness may be required to provide a doctor's note.

## 4. Parental Leave

### 4.1 Birth Parent Leave
Employees who give birth are eligible for **16 weeks of fully paid parental leave**, beginning on the birth date or up to 2 weeks before the expected due date.

### 4.2 Non-Birth Parent Leave
All other new parents (adoption, foster placement, non-birth parent) are eligible for **8 weeks of fully paid parental leave**.

### 4.3 Eligibility
- Must be employed at Contoso for at least **6 months** prior to the leave start date.
- Leave must commence within **12 months** of birth, adoption, or placement.

### 4.4 How to Request
Notify HR and your manager at least **30 days in advance** (where possible) by submitting the Parental Leave Request form in Workday.

## 5. Bereavement Leave

| Relationship | Paid Leave |
|---|---|
| Spouse, domestic partner, child | 5 days |
| Parent, sibling, grandparent | 3 days |
| Aunt, uncle, cousin, in-laws | 1 day |

Additional unpaid leave or PTO may be used with manager approval.

---

## 6. Jury Duty & Civic Leave

Employees summoned for jury duty will receive their regular pay for the duration of service. Employees must provide the summons notice to HR and their manager as soon as it is received.

---

## 7. Military Leave

Contoso complies with the Uniformed Services Employment and Reemployment Rights Act (USERRA). Employees on qualifying military leave will receive their regular salary for up to **4 weeks per year**. Benefits continue during this period.

---

## 8. Medical Leave (FMLA)

Eligible employees may take up to **12 weeks of unpaid, job-protected leave** under the Family and Medical Leave Act (FMLA) for qualifying events. Employees must have worked at Contoso for **at least 12 months** and **1,250 hours** in the preceding year.

Contact HR to initiate an FMLA request: hr@contoso.com or ext. 5100.
