# Contoso Corporation — 2025 Employee Benefits Guide
**Document:** HR-BEN-001  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025  
**Enrollment Deadline:** November 15, 2024 (Open Enrollment)  
**Owner:** Total Rewards Team, Human Resources

---

## Overview

Contoso is committed to supporting the health and financial wellbeing of our employees and their families. This guide summarizes the benefits available to full-time employees. Benefits become effective on your **first day of employment** for new hires joining outside of Open Enrollment.

To enroll or make changes, visit **Workday > Benefits** at workday.contoso.com.

---

## 1. Medical Insurance

Contoso partners with **Premera Blue Cross** to offer three medical plan options:

### Plan Comparison

| Plan | Deductible (Individual) | Deductible (Family) | Out-of-Pocket Max (Individual) | Monthly Premium (Employee) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Platinum PPO** | $500 | $1,000 | $2,000 | $180 |
| **Gold PPO** | $1,000 | $2,000 | $4,000 | $120 |
| **High Deductible Health Plan (HDHP)** | $1,500 | $3,000 | $6,000 | $60 |

- All plans include **preventive care at 100%** (no cost to employee)
- Prescription drug coverage included
- Mental health and substance use coverage included at parity with medical

**Contoso's Contribution:** Contoso covers **80% of the employee-only premium** for the Gold PPO plan. Dependent and other plan premiums are partially subsidized.

## 2. Dental Insurance

| Coverage Type | Plan Pays |
|---|---|
| Preventive (cleanings, x-rays) | 100% |
| Basic (fillings, extractions) | 80% |
| Major (crowns, bridges) | 50% |

**Employee Monthly Premium:** $18 (individual) / $48 (family)

## 3. Vision Insurance

Offered through **VSP**:
- **Annual Eye Exam:** Covered in full (once per year)
- **Frames/Lenses Allowance:** Up to $150 per year
- **Contact Lens Allowance:** Up to $150 per year (in lieu of glasses)

**Employee Monthly Premium:** $7 (individual) / $18 (family)

---

## 4. Health Savings Account (HSA)

Employees enrolled in the **HDHP** are eligible for a tax-advantaged HSA:

- **2025 IRS Contribution Limits:** $4,150 (individual) / $8,300 (family)
- **Contoso Contribution:** $750 (individual) / $1,500 (family) deposited in January
- Funds roll over year-to-year; account is yours to keep if you leave

---

## 5. Flexible Spending Accounts (FSA)

| Account Type | Annual Limit | Use For |
|---|---|---|
| Healthcare FSA | $3,050 | Medical, dental, vision expenses |
| Dependent Care FSA | $5,000 | Childcare, elder care |

FSA funds are "use it or lose it" (Healthcare FSA allows $610 rollover). Not available with HSA enrollment.

## 6. Life & Disability Insurance

### Basic Life Insurance
- **Coverage:** 2× annual salary (up to $500,000)
- **Cost:** Fully paid by Contoso

### Supplemental Life Insurance
- Employee may purchase additional coverage up to 5× salary
- Spouse/domestic partner coverage available up to $250,000
- Evidence of insurability may be required for amounts above guaranteed issue

### Short-Term Disability (STD)
- **Benefit:** 70% of base salary
- **Waiting Period:** 7 days
- **Duration:** Up to 12 weeks

### Long-Term Disability (LTD)
- **Benefit:** 60% of base salary
- **Begins:** After 90-day elimination period
- **Duration:** Until age 65 or recovery

## 7. Retirement — 401(k) Plan

Contoso offers a **401(k) plan** administered by **Fidelity Investments**.

- **Contoso Match:** 100% of first 4% of salary contributed, plus 50% of next 2%
- **Vesting:** Employer match vests over a **3-year graded schedule** (33%/66%/100%)
- **2025 Employee Contribution Limit:** $23,500 ($31,000 if age 50+)
- **Investment Options:** 20+ fund options including target-date funds
- **Enrollment:** Automatic enrollment at 3% on day 1; opt out or adjust anytime

## 8. Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

All employees and household members have access to **Lyra Health EAP** — free, confidential support:

- **Therapy & Counseling:** Up to 16 sessions per year at no cost
- **Financial Counseling:** 3 free sessions per year
- **Legal Consultation:** 1 free 30-minute consultation per issue
- **Crisis Support:** 24/7 phone and text support

**Access:** lyra.contoso.com | 1-800-555-0200
