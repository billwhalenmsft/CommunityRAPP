## Welcome to Contoso!

We are excited to have you join our team. This guide will walk you through your first 90 days at Contoso, covering what to expect, what you need to do, and who to contact when you need help.

**Your HR Onboarding Contact:** onboarding@contoso.com | (425) 555-0105

## Before Day 1 — Pre-Boarding Checklist

You will receive a **Welcome Email** from HR approximately 1 week before your start date. Please complete the following before arriving:

- [ ] Accept your offer letter (via DocuSign)
- [ ] Complete I-9 and tax forms (via Workday)
- [ ] Submit direct deposit information (via Workday)
- [ ] Set up your Contoso email (instructions in Welcome Email)
- [ ] Enroll in benefits (within 30 days of hire via workday.contoso.com)
- [ ] Order your laptop (IT will contact you separately)
- [ ] Complete background check (instructions emailed from Sterling)
- [ ] Review the Employee Handbook

## Your First 30 Days

### Week 1–2: Learn
- Complete all required trainings
- Set up 1:1 meetings with key team members (your manager will provide a list)
- Review your team's documentation and project wiki
- Attend team stand-ups and meetings
- Explore tools: Microsoft 365, Teams, Workday, Contoso internal systems

### Week 3–4: Connect
- Shadow experienced team members on key workflows
- Meet cross-functional partners (your manager will introduce you)
- Set up "coffee chats" with 3–5 people outside your immediate team
- Begin contributing to team tasks with manager guidance
- Complete your initial goal-setting in Workday (due Day 30)

**30-Day Check-In:** Your manager will schedule a formal 30-day check-in to discuss how things are going and answer questions.

---

## Days 31–60: Contribute

By day 60 you should:
- Be handling your core job responsibilities with increasing independence
- Have a clear understanding of your goals and success metrics
- Be actively participating in team meetings and contributing ideas
- Have completed your first project milestone or deliverable

**60-Day Check-In:** Your manager will review your progress against your 30-day goals and calibrate your 60-day targets.

---

## Days 61–90: Accelerate

By day 90 you should:
- Be fully ramped on your role and operating independently
- Have a strong network of internal relationships
- Be proposing improvements or ideas of your own
- Be tracking toward your annual performance goals

**90-Day Review:** Your manager will complete a formal 90-day review in Workday. This is not a full performance review but a milestone check to confirm successful completion of the introductory period.
