# Contoso Corporation — Anti-Harassment & Non-Discrimination Policy
**Document:** HR-POL-010  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025  
**Owner:** Human Resources & Legal

---

## Our Commitment

Contoso is committed to providing a workplace free of harassment, discrimination, and retaliation. Every employee, contractor, intern, and visitor deserves to be treated with dignity and respect. This is not just a legal requirement — it is a core expression of our values.

This policy applies to all work-related settings including offices, remote environments, business travel, company events, and digital communications.

---

## 1. Prohibited Conduct

### 1.1 Discrimination
Contoso prohibits adverse employment actions (hiring, firing, pay, promotion, assignments) based on any **protected characteristic**, including:

- Race, color, or national origin
- Sex, gender identity, or sexual orientation
- Age (40+)
- Religion or creed
- Disability (physical or mental)
- Veteran or military status
- Pregnancy or parental status
- Genetic information
- Any other characteristic protected by applicable federal, state, or local law

### 1.2 Harassment
Harassment is unwelcome conduct based on a protected characteristic that is severe or pervasive enough to create a hostile work environment. Examples include:

- Derogatory jokes, slurs, or stereotypes
- Unwanted physical contact
- Intimidating or bullying behavior
- Offensive images, memes, or communications shared digitally
- Exclusion from meetings or social activities based on a protected characteristic

### 1.3 Sexual Harassment
Sexual harassment includes:

- **Quid pro quo:** Conditioning employment benefits on submission to sexual conduct
- **Hostile environment:** Sexually charged jokes, images, innuendo, unwanted advances, or touching

Sexual harassment is prohibited regardless of the gender of the parties involved.

## 2. Reporting a Concern

Employees who experience or witness harassment, discrimination, or retaliation are encouraged to report it **promptly**. Contoso takes all reports seriously.

### Reporting Options:

| Method | Contact |
|---|---|
| **Direct Manager** | Appropriate when the manager is not the subject of the concern |
| **HR Business Partner** | Available via Teams @HRBizPartner |
| **HR Email** | hr@contoso.com |
| **Ethics Hotline (Anonymous)** | 1-800-555-ETHICS (available 24/7) or ethicshotline.contoso.com |
| **Legal/Compliance** | legal@contoso.com |

You do **not** need to report to your direct manager, especially if they are involved in the concern.

---

## 3. Investigation Process

Upon receiving a report, HR will:

1. **Acknowledge** the report within 1 business day
2. **Conduct a prompt, thorough investigation** — typically completed within 30 business days
3. **Interview** the reporting employee, the subject of the complaint, and relevant witnesses
4. **Maintain confidentiality** to the extent possible
5. **Take corrective action** if the investigation substantiates the complaint
6. **Inform** the reporting employee of the outcome (at an appropriate level of detail)

Both the reporting employee and the subject of the complaint have the right to share their perspective and will be treated with respect throughout the process.

---

## 4. Non-Retaliation

Contoso strictly prohibits retaliation against any employee who:
- Reports a concern in good faith
- Participates in an investigation
- Opposes conduct they reasonably believe violates this policy

Retaliation is itself a serious violation and will result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination — regardless of the outcome of the underlying complaint.

If you believe you are experiencing retaliation, report it immediately using the channels above.

---

## 5. Consequences

Violations of this policy are taken seriously. Depending on severity, consequences may include:

- Formal written warning
- Mandatory training
- Reassignment or transfer
- Demotion
- Termination of employment
- Legal referral where criminal conduct is involved

Contoso will not ignore substantiated reports.

---

## 6. Manager Responsibilities

Managers have a **heightened obligation** under this policy:

- Model respectful behavior at all times
- Intervene if they witness harassment or discriminatory conduct, even if no formal complaint is made
- Report concerns to HR — managers who know of a violation and fail to report it may be held responsible
- Never retaliate against employees for raising concerns
- Maintain strict confidentiality throughout any HR process

---

## 7. Training

All employees are required to complete **Anti-Harassment & Non-Discrimination Training** annually in the Contoso Learning Portal. New employees must complete this training within their first 5 business days.

Managers receive additional training on preventing, identifying, and responding to harassment.
