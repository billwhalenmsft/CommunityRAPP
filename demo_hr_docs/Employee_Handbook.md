## 3. Employment Policies

### 3.1 At-Will Employment
Employment at Contoso is at-will, meaning either party may end the relationship at any time, with or without cause, subject to applicable laws.

### 3.2 Equal Opportunity
Contoso is an equal opportunity employer. We do not discriminate based on race, color, religion, gender, age, national origin, disability, veteran status, or any other protected characteristic.

### 3.3 Background Checks
All new hires must pass a standard background check prior to their start date. Results are reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

### 3.4 Probationary Period
New employees complete a 90-day introductory period. Performance is reviewed at 30, 60, and 90 days.

## 4. Work Hours & Scheduling

### 4.1 Standard Hours
- Full-time: 40 hours/week (Monday–Friday, 8:00 AM–5:00 PM local time)
- Core hours: 10:00 AM–3:00 PM (flexible scheduling available outside core hours with manager approval)

### 4.2 Remote Work
Eligible roles may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Employees must maintain a secure, professional workspace and be reachable during core hours. Remote work agreements must be approved by HR and the direct manager.

### 4.3 Overtime
Non-exempt employees must obtain prior written approval before working overtime. Overtime is compensated at 1.5× the regular hourly rate per federal law.

---

## 5. Compensation & Payroll

### 5.1 Pay Schedule
Employees are paid **bi-weekly** (every other Friday). Direct deposit is strongly encouraged. Paper checks are available upon request.

### 5.2 Pay Grades
Contoso maintains a transparent pay band structure. Employees may request their pay band range from HR at any time.

### 5.3 Annual Reviews
Compensation reviews occur annually in **March**, aligned with performance evaluations. Merit increases range from 0–8% based on performance rating.

### 5.4 Bonuses
- **Annual Performance Bonus:** Up to 10% of base salary for eligible roles (paid in Q1 for prior year performance)
- **Spot Bonuses:** Managers may award up to $2,500 for exceptional contributions; requires VP approval

## 6. Code of Conduct

All employees are expected to:
- Treat colleagues, customers, and partners with respect
- Avoid conflicts of interest and disclose any potential conflicts to HR
- Never engage in bribery, corruption, or fraudulent activity
- Protect confidential company and customer information
- Report violations via the Ethics Hotline: 1-800-555-ETHICS or ethics@contoso.com

Violations may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.

---

## 7. Workplace Safety

Contoso is committed to a safe working environment. Employees must:
- Report injuries, near-misses, or unsafe conditions to their manager and HR immediately
- Complete annual safety training (available in the Learning Portal)
- Never report to work under the influence of alcohol or illegal substances

**Emergency:** Dial 911. Then notify building security at ext. 5555.

## 8. IT & Equipment Use

### 8.1 Acceptable Use
Company devices and systems are for business use. Limited personal use is permitted provided it does not interfere with work or violate policy.

### 8.2 Prohibited Activities
- Accessing, downloading, or sharing illegal, offensive, or adult content
- Installing unauthorized software
- Sharing login credentials

### 8.3 Equipment Return
All company property must be returned on or before the last day of employment.

---

## 9. Confidentiality & Data Privacy

Employees may not disclose confidential information (trade secrets, customer data, financial results, product roadmaps) to anyone outside the company without prior written authorization. This obligation survives termination of employment.

Contoso complies with GDPR, CCPA, and applicable data privacy laws. Questions? Contact privacy@contoso.com.
