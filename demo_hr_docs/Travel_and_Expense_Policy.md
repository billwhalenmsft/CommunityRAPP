# Contoso Corporation — Travel & Expense Policy
**Document:** HR-FIN-003  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025  
**Owner:** Finance & HR — jointly maintained

---

## Purpose

This policy provides guidelines for employees traveling on Contoso business and submitting expenses for reimbursement. All travel and expenses must be **business-justified**, **pre-approved** where required, and submitted within **30 days** of the expense.

Expense reports are submitted via **Concur** at concur.contoso.com.

---

## 1. Pre-Trip Approval

| Travel Type | Approval Required |
|---|---|
| Domestic (within the US) | Manager approval |
| International | Manager + VP + HR (minimum 4 weeks notice) |
| Travel over $5,000 total estimated cost | Manager + Finance Business Partner |

Book travel through **Contoso's preferred travel portal** (Concur Travel) to access negotiated rates and ensure compliance.

---

## 2. Air Travel

- **Domestic flights:** Economy class required for all flights under 6 hours
- **International flights:** Business class permitted for flights 6+ hours with manager approval
- Book at least **14 days in advance** when possible to secure best rates
- **Personal deviations** (extending trips for personal travel): The employee bears all additional cost; report must clearly separate business vs. personal portions

### Airline Loyalty Programs
Employees may keep personal frequent flyer miles earned on business travel.

---

## 3. Ground Transportation

| Method | Policy |
|---|---|
| **Rental Car** | Compact/mid-size preferred; SUV/luxury requires justification; always decline collision damage waiver (Contoso's corporate insurance covers this) |
| **Rideshare/Taxi** | Reimbursable; use most cost-effective option |
| **Personal Vehicle** | Reimbursed at IRS standard mileage rate ($0.67/mile for 2025); includes gas and wear |
| **Public Transit** | Always reimbursable; preferred for short urban trips |
| **Airport Parking** | Economy/off-site parking preferred; on-site parking reimbursable if cost-justified |

---

## 4. Lodging

- Book hotels through Contoso's preferred vendors in Concur Travel
- **Per diem rates:**

| City Tier | Nightly Maximum |
|---|---|
| Tier 1 (NYC, SF, Boston, DC) | $350/night |
| Tier 2 (Chicago, LA, Seattle, Austin) | $275/night |
| Tier 3 (all other US cities) | $200/night |
| International | Market rate; pre-approval required for anything above $350/night |

- Hotel loyalty points may be kept by the employee
- Personal upgrades are the employee's responsibility

---

## 5. Meals & Incidentals

| Meal | Per Diem Maximum |
|---|---|
| Breakfast | $20 |
| Lunch | $25 |
| Dinner | $60 |
| **Daily Total** | **$105** |

- Meals with clients or team members (3+ people) may exceed per diem with manager approval and business justification
- **Alcohol:** Reasonable alcohol at business meals is reimbursable; personal alcohol is not
- Room service is included in meal per diem; minibar is not reimbursable

---

## 6. Business Entertainment

Customer or partner entertainment (dinners, events, tickets) requires:
- **Pre-approval** from manager and VP for amounts over $500/event
- **Business purpose** documented in Concur (who attended, business outcome)
- Attendance of at least one Contoso employee required

| Entertainment Type | Limit |
|---|---|
| Client meals | $150/person |
| Sporting/entertainment events | $250/person (tickets + incidentals) |
| Holiday gifts to clients | $75/person (must comply with customer's gift policy) |

Gifts to Contoso employees from vendors must be reported to HR if over $50 in value.

---

## 7. Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following are **not reimbursable**:

- Personal entertainment (movies, spa, gym during travel)
- Travel for personal reasons or personal portion of combined trips
- Traffic/parking tickets or fines
- Lost or stolen personal items
- Pet care while traveling
- Alcohol consumed alone (not at a business meal)
- Travel insurance (covered by Contoso's corporate policy)
- First-class upgrades unless approved in advance

---

## 8. Corporate Credit Card

Employees at Level 5+ may request a **Contoso Corporate Mastercard** through Finance. Use of the corporate card:
- Is required for all airfare and hotel bookings over $500
- Charges must be reconciled monthly in Concur
- Personal charges on the corporate card are prohibited and may result in disciplinary action

To request a card: finance@contoso.com with manager approval.

---

## 9. Submitting Expense Reports

1. Submit via **Concur** within **30 days** of expense date (end of trip for travel)
2. Attach all **original receipts** for expenses over $25
3. Provide business justification for each expense line
4. Manager approval is required before Finance processing
5. Reimbursement is processed via payroll within **10 business days** of approval

**Late submissions** (over 90 days) require VP approval and may not be reimbursed.

---

## 10. International Travel Considerations

- **Travel vaccinations/medications** related to business travel are reimbursable
- **Visa fees** and passport renewal fees (if travel-required) are reimbursable
- **Currency exchange fees** are reimbursable; submit with receipts
- **International calling/data plans:** Employees should obtain an international plan or use Teams; reasonable roaming charges are reimbursable

Contact HR before international travel for health & safety briefings if traveling to elevated-risk regions.
