💡 The Story We're Telling (Updated)

We start in the customer's world — the fragmented, multi-system reality Customer Care Associates face every day. Then we put them inside a unified experience where every channel, every customer interaction, and every piece of context lives in one place. We go deep on what's DIFFERENT: native telephony with real-time AI, guided process flows, tiered routing from ERP data, and a single platform that Sales and Service share without connectors. By the end, they're not comparing features — they're imagining their team working differently.

Intent Agent — Already seen in action during the phone call. Listens to conversation, suggests next steps using KB, case history, and product data. Guides associate to faster resolution in real time.
Cases
Case Management Agent — Monitors case queue. Auto-sends follow-up nudge emails after 48 hours of customer silence. Suggests closing cases that match known resolutions. Can handle routine responses without human intervention.

Knowledge Management Agent — Monitors case patterns, identifies KB gaps. If 15 cases came in about the same bottle filler issue with no KB article, it drafts one and routes for review. Builds the internal KB that doesn't exist today.
