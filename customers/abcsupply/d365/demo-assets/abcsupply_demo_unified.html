📖 The Demo Story — A Day in the Life at ABC Supply Savage

Setting: You are Josh Henriksen, one of two service coordinators at ABC Supply's Savage Branch. You and Aaron run all service intake, scheduling, and dispatch for a 3-tech field team serving window and door contractors across the south metro Twin Cities. Today you're running everything out of D365 Customer Service + Field Service — a far cry from the Smartsheet + copy/paste + personal texts that run the operation today.

The Challenge ABC Supply has today:

  • Service requests come in by phone, email, and a web form — each goes into Smartsheet manually
  • No screen pop when contractors call — you're looking up their info while they wait
  • Dispatch is all manual — texts from personal phones, no schedule visibility
  • Parts status calls flood the phones — no proactive notifications to customers
  • Techs carry no documentation standard — photos are optional, paper-based
  • No completion report — contractors get a phone call, homeowners get nothing
  • Anticipating 5x growth in 2027 — cannot scale this on Smartsheet

Today you will see: How D365 CS + Field Service unifies the entire work order lifecycle — from first call to completion report — with zero copy/paste, automated SMS, and mobile-first field tech tools.
